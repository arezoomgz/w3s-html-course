coffee

Coffee is a globally beloved beverage known for its rich aroma, robust flavor, and stimulating effects. Originating from Ethiopia, coffee has a long history that spans centuries and cultures, evolving from a traditional drink in the Arab world to a modern staple enjoyed in various forms worldwide. Made from the roasted seeds of the Coffea plant, coffee can be prepared in numerous ways, including espresso, drip, French press, and more, each method offering a unique taste experience. It's not just a drink but a cultural phenomenon, fostering social interaction in cafes and coffeehouses, and providing a moment of pause in daily routines.
