The Samoyed dog, often called a "Sammie," is a breed known for its friendly and gentle disposition, making it an excellent family pet. Originating from Siberia, these dogs were initially used by the Samoyede people for herding reindeer, pulling sleds, and keeping their owners warm in the harsh Arctic conditions. Characterized by their thick, fluffy, white coats and a distinctive "Sammy smile," Samoyeds are not only beautiful but also incredibly resilient and hardworking.
